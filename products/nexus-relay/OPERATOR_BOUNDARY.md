# Operator Boundary

NEXUS Relay is for lawful retrieval of public resources the operator is permitted to access. It must not be configured to evade authentication, CAPTCHAs, technical access controls, private-account restrictions, paywalls, or platform bans. Public availability does not remove privacy, copyright, contractual, or data-protection obligations.

Operators should add authentication before exposing Relay publicly, define retention limits, respect robots and source policies where applicable, and restrict high-volume collection to reviewed domains.
